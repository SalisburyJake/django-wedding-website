Livonia

Getting There and Away

For those coming in form outo of town, welcome!

Livonia is located about 30 minutes from downtown Detroit and also 25 minutes from the Detroit Metropolitan Airport (DTW).

Where to Stay

Things to Do

There's no shortage of things to do in metro-Detroit! Some ideas are listed below.

Downtown Detroit

If you have some extra time there is a lot to do at Campus Martius. list of things to do ie the basketball court area in the summer and decorations. A short walk away is the fist and liberty statue. There's also the Detroit Riverwalk blah blah blah. Belle Isle blah blah blah

Downtown Farmington

A walk around downtown Farmington and enjoy the farmer's market on Saturdays! Enjoy brunch at one of our favorite resturants MI.mosa! (The chicken and waffles are SOOOO good) If you're up for some drinks Farmington Brewing Company and Up Arcade Bar!

Downtown Plymouth

A walk around downtown Plymouth and enjoy the scenery and fountain! If you're looking for a place to eat Barrios has the best tacos!!

Ann Arbor

Some people like flowers. If you’re one of them, go here. There’s even a butterfly conservatory.

Shopping

The closest mall to our venue is Twelve Oaks in Novi, but if you are looking for something more upscale we recommend the Somerset Collection in Troy. If you're looking for an outlet mall Great Lakes Crossing in Auburn Hills!
